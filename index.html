Professional Introduction

My name is Leah Kotsmith, and I am an Accounting major at the University of Minnesota Duluth with a minor in English. I have maintained a 3.9 GPA and have earned Dean's List honors every semester. Along with my coursework in financial, managerial, cost, and intermediate accounting, I gained professional experience as a receptionist at Great Clips, where I developed strong organizational, communication, and leadership skills. I am eager to apply my detail orientation, analytical ability, and communication skills in an internship setting this summer to continue growing as an accounting professional.
