Discover UMD & Duluth

Why I Chose UMD

Coming from Minneapolis, I wanted a college experience that was both close to home and far enough away to feel like a new adventure. The University of Minnesota Duluth stood out to me because of its beautiful location near Lake Superior, its welcoming campus, and its strong academic reputation. I also liked that UMD is connected to the larger University of Minnesota system, which means access to great resources while still enjoying the smaller, more personal feel of Duluth's campus. The size felt just right—not overwhelming, but big enough to have plenty of opportunities to get involved.

What I Like About Duluth and the Surrounding Area

Duluth itself is an incredible city. Between the lake views, the Canal Park boardwalk, and the outdoorsy feel of the area, there's always something to do. Lake Superior is one of my favorite parts because it changes with the seasons, and walking along the shore is both relaxing and inspiring. Just outside the city, places like Two Harbors and the North Shore offer hiking, waterfalls, and amazing views. Having access to both city life and natural beauty makes Duluth special.

Personal Experiences and Campus Highlights

Living on campus has been one of the best parts of my college experience so far. I've made such close friends in my on-campus apartments, and being part of clubs has helped me connect with people who share similar interests. Attending football and hockey games is always a highlight because there's so much school spirit, and it really brings students together. Whether it's grabbing food with friends after class, walking by the lake, or exploring new places downtown, I've found that UMD and Duluth provide a balance of community, fun, and opportunities to grow.
